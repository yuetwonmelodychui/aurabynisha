About Us

About the Creator

Nisha is the heart and hands behind AURA. Born with a love for fabric, texture, and the quiet power of modest fashion, she started AURA with a single belief — that every woman deserves to feel seen, celebrated, and beautiful in what she wears. Each piece is a reflection of her roots, her culture, and her passion for craftsmanship.

Mission Statement

At AURA, every piece tells a story. Our hijabs and shawls are 100% original designs — no mass production, no shortcuts. Each one is thoughtfully handcrafted by skilled women artisans in Pakistan, who bring generations of textile tradition into every stitch, fold, and finish.

We are committed to sustainability. Our fabrics are sourced responsibly, our process is slow and intentional, and our packaging is designed with the earth in mind. We believe that fashion can be both beautiful and conscious — and that the two should never have to compete.

Behind every piece is a woman who made it with her hands, and a woman who will wear it with pride. AURA exists to honour both. When you choose AURA, you are not just buying a product — you are supporting independent craftsmanship, empowering women, and carrying a piece of something real.
